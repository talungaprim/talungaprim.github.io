SYMPOMS FOR COMMON DISEASES
Disease Signs and Symptoms

Diabetes

Diabetes is a lifelong condition that causes a person's blood glucose (sugar) level to become too high.
  • Feeling very thirsty
  • Urinating more frequently than usual, particularly at night
  • Weight loss and loss of muscle bulk
  • Cuts or wounds that heal slowly
  • Blurred vision

Malaria 

Malaria causes uncomfortable symptoms and can lead to life-threatening or life-changing complications.
  • Fever
  • Dry cough
  • Tiredness
  • Aches and pains
  • Loss of taste or smell

Covid- 19

Affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.
